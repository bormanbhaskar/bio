About

Securing what matters, with precision.

I am a results-driven cybersecurity leader with 14 plus years of progressive experience building, scaling, and managing global information security programs. My work spans product security, penetration testing, secure SDLC, DevSecOps, risk management, and cloud infrastructure security across AWS and Azure — balancing strategic leadership with hands-on technical execution.

As Sr. Manager, Cybersecurity at Dexcom, I lead security initiatives across product and enterprise environments, driving product security aligned with regulatory standards including FDA, PCI DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001, UL2900, and HIPAA. I've also contributed to the global security community by responsibly disclosing vulnerabilities — registering 20+ CVE identifiers and earning recognition in multiple public Hall of Fame programs.

Based in Bengaluru, India, I hold a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science from Rajasthan Technical University. I'm passionate about fostering a security-first culture, mentoring the next generation of security professionals, and continuously pushing the boundaries of what "secure" means.
