About

Securing what matters, with precision.

I am a results-driven cybersecurity leader with over 14 years of experience in building and leading enterprise security programs. My work spans offensive security, vulnerability research, cloud security, and governance — from hands-on penetration testing to shaping organisation-wide security strategy.

Over the years I've contributed to the global security community by discovering and responsibly disclosing vulnerabilities in products from D-Link, Tesla, Symantec, and others — resulting in 10 CVEs published in the National Vulnerability Database. I believe security is as much about people and process as it is about technology.

Based in Bengaluru, India, I hold a B.Tech and M.Tech from Karnataka State Open University, Mysuru. I'm passionate about mentoring the next generation of security professionals and continuously pushing the boundaries of what "secure" means.
